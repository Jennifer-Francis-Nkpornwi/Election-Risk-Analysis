# Project Brief

## Background

Election monitoring organizations often rely on historical election results and observer reports to identify areas that may be vulnerable to electoral irregularities. However, these datasets are frequently analyzed separately, making it difficult to obtain a comprehensive view of election risk.

## Business Problem

How can historical polling unit data and election observer incident reports be combined to identify Local Government Areas (LGAs) that may require increased monitoring during future elections?

## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to:

* Merge polling unit election data with election observer incident reports.
* Identify relationships between voting anomalies and reported incidents.
* Develop a Risk Score model to estimate electoral risk at the LGA level.
* Build an interactive dashboard to support election monitoring and decision-making.

## Tools Used

* Excel Power Query
* Power BI
* DAX
* Excel

## Expected Outcome

Provide election stakeholders with a data-driven approach for identifying high-risk LGAs and allocating monitoring resources more effectively.
