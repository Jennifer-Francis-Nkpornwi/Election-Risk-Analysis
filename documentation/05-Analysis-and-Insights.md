# Analysis and Key Insights

## Highest Risk LGAs

The analysis identified the following LGAs as having the highest electoral risk scores:

1. Nassarawa
2. Port-Harcourt
3. Surulere

These LGAs recorded elevated anomaly rates, incident severity scores, and incident frequencies.

## Improvement Analysis:

A comparison between 2019 and 2023 Risk Scores was conducted.

`RiskDifference = RiskScore(2023) - RiskScore(2019)`

## Most Improved LGAs

1. Port-Harcourt
2. Surulere
3. Nassarawa

These LGAs recorded lower risk scores in 2023 than in 2019.

## Relationship Between Anomalies and Incidents

The scatter plot analysis showed a relationship between voting anomalies and incident reports across several LGAs.

LGAs with higher anomaly rates often recorded higher incident frequencies and severity scores.

## Recommendation:

INEC should prioritize monitoring resources, election observers, and security deployments to LGAs identified as high risk by the model.

## Limitation:

The analysis depends on reported incidents and available election records.

Unreported incidents and data quality issues may affect the accuracy of the risk estimates.
